Hello World – Introducing 500 words or less

Last updated on Apr 21, 2021

Empirical laws, scientific theories and concepts are often hard to digest, particularly for the general public, or if you are just starting out in the field.

Over the next few months, I will use this platform to introduce a range of concepts in my field (palaeobiology/macroecology) via short blog posts. To ensure these are useful to a broad audience, I will limit these posts to 500 words or less, and avoid jargon wherever possible… the thing we all hate most! I hope these posts will prove insightful and help to improve your understanding without diving deep into the literature… I am sure I will learn a thing or two along the way!

Until then… remember to be kind to yourself and others… these are difficult times (#@$! you COVID).

Lewis A. Jones, PhD
Palaeobiology & Macroecology

My research interests include palaeobiology, macroecology, and fossil record bias.
